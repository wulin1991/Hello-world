De Young Kids

De Young Kids is an App facing to the children who are visiting the De Young Museum of Art.

Parents would all like their children show a native talent in art. As you know children love science and history museum better than art museums. The fact is children need to encourage and help to appreciate art. So the problem is children feel bored and attention span when they’re viewing the actual art, and when they feel bored they might not behave well in the museum.

The De Young Kids will provides an amusing visit experence for children, help them understand the process and background of actual arts. For parents, they can get hands-free on children.
